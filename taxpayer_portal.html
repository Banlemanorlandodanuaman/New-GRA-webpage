Frequently Asked Questions

What is a TIN number?

A Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) is a unique number assigned to every taxpayer registered with GRA. It is used to identify you in all tax transactions.

How do I file my PAYE returns?

Click "File Your Returns" in the top navigation, then click "FILE NOW" next to the pending PAYE period. Fill in the form and submit.

What happens if I miss a filing deadline?

Late filing attracts a penalty of GHS 500 or 3 times the tax payable, whichever is higher. File as soon as possible to minimize penalties.

How do I get a Tax Clearance Certificate?

You must have all outstanding returns filed and payments made. Go to "Your Tax Clearance" in the sidebar and click "Request Clearance".
