New Taxpayer Registration

Register an individual or organisation as a new taxpayer and generate a TIN instantly.

  • Individual or Organisation
  • Ghana Card verification
  • All 16 regions of Ghana
  • Auto-generated TIN number
  • Downloadable TIN Certificate
  • SOAP web service integration

New Vehicle Registration (VIT)

Register a commercial vehicle for Vehicle Income Tax and generate a tax certificate.

  • All vehicle categories
  • Auto VIT fee calculation
  • Owner TIN verification
  • Barcode on certificate
  • Downloadable tax certificate
  • Quarterly payment tracking
